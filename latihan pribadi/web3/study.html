About

Marsella is a 2nd year student in Information Systems at Binasarana Informatics University. She comes from Majalengka, a small town on the outskirts of Cirebon. now in Bekasi, she lives with her aunt and uncle. she has a hobby of reading and watching movies. her favorite sport is playing volleyball
when she was in high school she joined several organizations, namely scouts and nature lovers. in the scout organization in the logistics division, she also participated in national-level competitions with her group friends to represent the school. and in her own community, she took part in cross-terrain and cultural competitions and won 2nd place in the Rector's Cup at Gunung Jati University
